Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How often should I service my Lamborghini in Dubai?

Typically every 10,000 km or once a year, whichever comes first. Performance driving may require more frequent checks.

Can I service my Lamborghini outside an official dealership?

Yes, as long as the service center follows Lamborghini standards and uses approved tools and parts.

How long does Lamborghini servicing take?

Minor services may take 1 day, while major services or diagnostics can take 2-3 days.

Does regular servicing improve resale value?

Absolutely. A well-documented service history significantly increases resale value.

Is diagnostics included in Lamborghini servicing?

Yes, comprehensive diagnostics are a core part of professional Lamborghini maintenance.
